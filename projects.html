Hydrocyclone

PROJECT TYPE: SCHOOL

In a group of 5, we were tasked to design a device that would increase accessibility to clean water and find the monetary and non-monetary costs associated with it. The hydrocyclone we designed filters out large particles when contaminated water is pumped through it. Particles with higher density will remain in the bottom of the device and cleaner water will escape from the top. Water that comes out need to be put through a water filter before consumption but the device can increase the lifespan of water filters.
